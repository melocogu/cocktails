Manhattan

Ingredients:

  • 60ml rye bourbon
  • 30ml vermouth (martini rossi)
  • 3 dashes of angustura
  • 1 dash orange bitter

Instructions:

Step 1: Rub an orange peel around the inside of the glass, then add ice on a mixing glass (not a shaker).

Step 2: Combine all ingredients in the glass and stir gently.

Step 2: Strain to a whiskey glass, garnish with a cherry on a toothpick, and serve.
