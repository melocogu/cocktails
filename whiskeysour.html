Whiskey Sour

Ingredients:

  • 60ml bourbon (Bulleit or Woodford)
  • 30ml lemon juice
  • 30ml sugar syrup
  • 10ml egg white
  • 3 dashes angostura bitters

Instructions:

Step 1: Add everything to a shaker and dry shake (no ice).

Step 2: Add ice and shake again. Double strain into a whiskey glass.

Step 3: Garnish with an orange peel and cherry.
