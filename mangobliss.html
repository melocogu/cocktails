Mango Bliss

Ingredients:

  • 30ml vodka
  • 30ml mango liqueur
  • 20ml elderflower liqueur
  • 15ml lemon juice
  • 10ml elderflower syrup
  • 30ml orange juice

Instructions:

Step 1: Shake all ingredients with ice.

Step 2: Strain into a cocktail glass.

Step 4: Garnish with mint.
