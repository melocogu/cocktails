Southern Sunset

Ingredients:

  • 30ml grenadine
  • 60ml lemon juice
  • 30ml sugar syrup
  • Orange Juice
  • Pineapple Juice

Instructions:

Step 1: Shake grenadine, sugar syrup, and lemon juice with ice.

Step 2: Strain into a glass, add ice, and top with orange and pineapple juice.

Step 3: Garnish with an orange slice.
