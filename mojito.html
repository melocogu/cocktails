Classic Mojito

Ingredients:

  • Half a lime sliced in 4
  • 2 spoons raw sugar
  • 5cl light rum
  • 10-12 mint leaves
  • 7up or club soda

Instructions:

Step 1: Muddle lime and sugar in a glass.

Step 2: Add mint leaves and muddle again.

Step 3: Add crushed ice and top with 7up or club soda.

Step 4: Garnish with a mint sprig and lime wheel.
