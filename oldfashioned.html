Old Fashioned

Ingredients:

  • 1 bar spoon sugar
  • 4 dashes angostura bitter
  • 60ml rye bourbon

Instructions:

Step 1: Combine the sugar and bitters in a glass and lightly mash them together. Add ice and bourbon, then stir well.

Step 2: Lightly burn an orange peel, hold it near the glass, and squeeze it while burning before placing it in the glass.

Step 2: Take another orange peel and rub it around the rim of the glass.
