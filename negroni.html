Negroni

Ingredients:

  • 30ml Campari
  • 30ml sweet vermouth (Martini Rosso)
  • 30ml gin
  • Orange slices

Instructions:

Step 1: Fill a whiskey glass with ice.

Step 2: Add all ingredients to the glass.

Step 3: Stir with a bar spoon until chilled.

Step 4: Garnish with an orange slice.
