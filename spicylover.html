Spicy Lover

Ingredients:

  • 30ml dark rum (Bacardi)
  • 15ml Passoa
  • 15ml lime juice
  • 15ml chili syrup
  • 15ml sugar syrup
  • ginger ale

Instructions:

Step 1: Shake rum, passoa, lime juice, and both syrups with ice.

Step 2: Strain into a cocktail glass.

Step 3: Add fresh ice and top with ginger ale.

Step 4: Garnish with a lime wheel.
